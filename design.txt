"escape the room" text based puzzle game

variables:
	player 
		deaths(specific deaths used already)
		hearts(deaths left to win)

	Room 
		contents(every interactable in the room)
		Inventory(items)
		items(everything in room)
		location(what item player is at)
methods:
	Room 
		change room 
		Inspect item (looks at specified item)
		Use item (use item from inventory) 
		grab item(picks up pick ups)
	Inventory
		Use item(uses specific item)
		View item (gives details about specific item)
		combine(merges 2 listed items into one)
